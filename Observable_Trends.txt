**Analysis of Heroes of Pymoli Data

+ 576 players (84% male, 14% female, 2% other or not disclosed)
+ 179 items, avg price $3.05
+ 780 purchases, $2,379.99 in revenue


**Trends observed in the data:

   While males make up 84% of the player population, they spend the least money per purchase.

   Almost 45% of players are in the age range of 20-24, with almost 77% being in the age range of 15-29. Only about 5% of players are below 10 or over 40.

   The top 5 items by total purchase value make up almost 10% of the total revenue across all 179 items.
